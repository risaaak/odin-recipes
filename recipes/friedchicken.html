Fried Chicken Recipe

Fried Chicken

A chicken that is fried

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cornstarch
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • White Pepper
  • Ground Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Mix equal part of cornstarch and flour to create a breading mix enough to coat all the chicken
  3. Add salt, garlic powder, paprika, white pepper, ground pepper into the breading mix
  4. Coat the chicken complete with the breading mix and remove the excess breading mix
  5. Fry the chicken in the hot oil until golden brown
  6. Serve
