Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti

An italian pasta dish with tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat pan with olive oil and boil water in another pan
  2. Mince garlic and onion
  3. Saute garlic and onion
  4. Add ground beef to the pan and brown
  5. Add tomato sauce
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Cook pasta in the boiling water according to the package instruction
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with tomato sauce
  9. Serve
