Chicken Pastel Recipe

Chicken Pastel

A filipino chicken dish

Ingredients

  • Chicken Fillet Strips
  • Cream
  • Green Peas
  • Whole Kernel Corn
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mince onion and garlic
  2. Saute onion and garlic in a hot pan
  3. Add the chicken fillet strips to the hot pan
  4. Add cream
  5. Add green peas and whole kernel corn to the pan
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Serve
